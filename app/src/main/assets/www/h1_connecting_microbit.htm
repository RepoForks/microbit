Connecting your micro:bit

1. From the homescreen, tap the 'Connect to your micro:bit' button.



2. Next, tap the 'Connect a new micro:bit' button.



3. Read the displayed help text and, when you are ready to continue tap the green icon.



4. Whilst hold downing buttons A and B, press and then release the reset button.

5. The micro:bit should enter pairing mode and 'BlueZone' should be displayed.

6. After 'BlueZone' has been displayed a pattern will be shown on your micro:bit. This code is unique to your micro:bit. Keep it secret!



7. Press the squares in the grid displayed on your mobile or tablet device so that they match what is displayed on the micro:bit.

8. When you're ready, press the green button to continue.



9. The app will now search for the micro:bit



10. Once the micro:bit has been discovered, it's name will be shown.

11. Rename the micro:bit if you wish.

12. When you are ready, press the green button to continue.



13. Congratulations, you've now paired your micro:bit



14. Next it's time to get coding so that you can make that micro:bit do something!
