Flashing a project to your micro:bit

1. Ensure you have previously created a project. If not, please see the 'Creating a Project' help section.

2. From the Homescreen, tap on the 'saved projects' button.



2. All projects that have been downloaded to your device are displayed in a scrollable list.



3. Scroll to the project you'd like to flash.

4. Tap it once to expand (mobile devices only).



5. Ensure your micro:bit is connected, if not please read the Connecting your micro:bit tutorial.

6. Tap the 'flash' button.



7. Read the displayed help text and when you're ready, tap the green button.



7. The application will now attempt to find your micro:bit



8. Once the micro:bit has been discovered, a helper screen will be displayed. Tap the A or B button on your micro:bit to continue.



9. The application will present you with help text to inform you of the progress now syncing with your micro:bit.



9. Once the following message is displayed, the micro:bit will be flashed with your code.
10. WARNING - Do NOT turn off the micro:bit or disconnect the app during this process.



11. Congratulations, your program has now been flashed to your micro:bit
